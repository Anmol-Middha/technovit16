InformalMATH-E-TRONICS

As we move forward towards new technology and inventions, and with the idea of smart city driving us to a better tomorrow, there is a need to learn a smart way to design our circuits to meet their needs. For those aspiring for circuit designing, but have no clue where to start, Mathetronics is your place to be. A great way to stay “connected’, it is a two day tutorial class workshop, the first day being about the basics of circuit design, and the second an on-screening and team selection (based on a 3 round competition). Teams of two will go through a three questionnaire rounds, the first two being 10 questions in 30 minutes. The last round will have 10 questions for a time period of 60 minutes. Selection of the team will be based on successfully solving of the problem in the shortest time period. Periodic eliminations will take place between the rounds, sending only half its competitors onwards, and 4 teams for the final round.

InformalROBOMAZE

The event is a race event where the fastest line follower bot will be awarded the first prize. It consists of two rounds where in each team is given single try in each round. The first round is before lunch and the next will be after the lunch. Each team may consist maximum of 4 members.
The winner will be decided on who completes the track fastest. The track’s image is given below. Track width is 30mm. The starting point and the finishing points are represented as ‘S’ and ‘F’. Each team has a balance of 50 points (subject to change). These points will be considered in case when there is a tie. Track consists of two checkpoints. They are represented as ‘C1’ and ‘C2’. The bots must pass through C1 and C2. Crossing these checkpoints would result in awarding of 10 points.
The race will be considered as a tie if the time taken by two or more teams is same (highly unlikely), or if the team is not able to complete it in the allotted time (8 mins). The points will be deducted on these basis: If the bot goes offline – 5 points Per touch – 2 points If the bot takes wrong path – 7 points In case of tie, the above criteria will follow and the winner will be the one with more points.
